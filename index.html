About Me

My interest in software development spawns from my creative nature. The Arts are my life-long passion and I dabble in many mediums with my favorites being painting, ink, poetry, and theatre.

I did not enjoy the stress of surviving on my artistic talents and changed my focus to business administration. From there I discovered the creativity in computers! Software development compliments my creative personality and problem solving skills.

Outside of my educational goals my free time is spent swimming, running, painting, and writing. When I need a break from studying, I like to play my favorite guitar.

I'm also an appreciator of good jokes and my least favorite writing topic is ME!
